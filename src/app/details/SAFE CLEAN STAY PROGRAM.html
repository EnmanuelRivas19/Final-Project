For more than 30 years, the safety of our customers and employees has always been our number one priority and that is why today more than ever we have created our SAFE CLEAN STAY program that implements COVID-19 policies and procedures on our properties in order to create an environment where our clients feel safe but at the same time enjoying the exclusivities we offer.

  • We have a specially trained and POSI certified staff.
  • When you enter our facilities, you must fill out a POSI check, a solution for the prevention of the spread of COVID-19.
  • Cleaning, disinfection, and protection are words that cannot be missed when it comes to keeping our clients safe.
  • An exhaustive disinfection and ozone treatment is performed before guests arrive and after they leave.
  • Safety and hygiene guidelines are followed.
  • Floors marked for social distancing
  • Hand sanitizer available to guests & staff
  • Stay-at-home policy for staff with symptoms
  • Regularly sanitized high-traffic area
  • Staff required to regularly wash hands
  • Daily temperature and symptom checks for staff
  • Contactless check-in and check-out
  • Individually wrapped toiletries
